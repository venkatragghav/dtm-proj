    about me



    Hey, I’m Lionel, a 26-year-old footballer from Argentina with a deep love for the game. Football has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember, and I’ve spent years honing my skills as a forward. My game is all about creativity, vision, and control—whether it’s dribbling through tight spaces, setting up my teammates, or finding the back of the net. I’m always striving to improve and play the game with passion. I’m looking to connect with other footballers, join local matches, and keep growing as a player. Whether it’s a friendly game, a competitive tournament, or just sharing tips and tricks, I’m up for it. Football is more than a sport for me—it’s how I express myself and challenge my limits.
