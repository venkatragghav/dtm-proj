    About Us

    We are a passionate four-member team united by our love for sports and technology, dedicated to creating a sports networking platform that connects athletes and sports enthusiasts. Each member brings unique skills: a tech-savvy developer focused on user experience, a sports strategist researching user needs, a creative marketer driving outreach, and a diligent project manager ensuring smooth collaboration. Through our Design Thinking and Methodology (DTM) project, we've designed a platform that simplifies organizing sports activities and fosters a sense of community among players. Our combined efforts aim to enhance the sports experience for users, making it easier to find games and connect with fellow athletes. We are excited to share our project and contribute to the vibrant world of sports.
